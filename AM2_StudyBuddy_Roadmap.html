⭐ EXAM INTEL
Most Important Exam Topics

⭐ Unit 1 – Must Do

  • Cauchy–Riemann Equations (Cartesian & Polar) – always asked
  • Analytic Functions – concept + finding harmonic conjugate
  • Cauchy's Integral Theorem & Formula – guaranteed 5 marks
  • Roots of Complex Numbers / Roots of Unity
  • Logarithm of Complex Variable – principal value numericals
  • Euler's Formula, de Moivre's Theorem

⭐ Unit 2 – Must Do

  • Laurent Series Expansion – in given regions
  • Residue Theorem – finding residues at poles
  • Residue Integration of Real Integrals – contour type
  • Bilinear / Möbius Transformations – finding map of regions
  • Conformal Mapping – basic concept & angle preservation

⭐ Unit 3 – Must Do

  • Inverse Laplace Transform – partial fractions & convolution
  • Second Shifting Theorem (Heaviside)
  • Fourier Series – arbitrary period, even/odd functions
  • Half-Range Fourier Expansions – sine & cosine series
  • Fourier Transform & Inverse Fourier Transform
  • Convolution Theorem (both Laplace & Fourier)

⭐ Unit 4 – Must Do

  • Wave Equation – separation of variables
  • Heat Equation – 1D with Fourier series
  • D'Alembert's Solution
  • Dirichlet Problem / 2D Laplace
  • PDEs via Laplace Transform
🔥 HIGH WEIGHTAGE
High Weightage Numerical Topics

🔥 Complex Analysis Numericals

  1. C–R Equations – checking analyticity & harmonic conjugate
  2. Roots of Unity – cube roots, 4th roots, nth roots
  3. Cauchy's Integral Formula – contour around poles
  4. Laurent Series – expanding in annular region
  5. Residues at poles – simple, double, essential
  6. Möbius maps – finding image of regions / circles
  7. Real integrals via residue – ∫(rational) & ∫(trig)
  8. Log of complex numbers – principal value & branch cuts

🔥 Laplace Transform Numericals

  1. L{t·f(t)}, L{f(t)/t} – differentiation & integration of transforms
  2. First Shifting Theorem – e^at·f(t) type
  3. Second Shifting Theorem – Heaviside U(t-a)·f(t-a)
  4. Inverse L.T. via partial fractions
  5. Convolution theorem – integral equation type
  6. Solving ODEs by Laplace – 2nd order IVPs
  7. Dirac delta function – L{δ(t-a)}
  8. Fourier series – f(x) in [0, 2π] & arbitrary period

🔥 PDE Numericals

  1. Wave equation – separation of variables + boundary conditions
  2. D'Alembert's formula – direct application
  3. Heat equation – u_t = c²u_xx type
  4. Fourier series solutions of BVPs
  5. Laplace equation in rectangle – Dirichlet BVP
  6. PDEs via Laplace transform
  7. Half-range Fourier expansions
